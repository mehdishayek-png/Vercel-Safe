# Midas Match — Impeccable Design Context

## Target Audience
Job seekers in India (primarily tech/operations professionals aged 22-35) who want AI-powered job matching that goes beyond basic keyword search. They're tech-savvy but time-constrained.

## Use Cases
- Upload resume and get AI-matched job listings from 8+ sources
- Deep analysis of individual job fit with salary estimates
- Track saved jobs and applications in one place
- Generate cover letters and interview prep

## Brand Personality / Tone
**Bold, confident, and warm** — not cold or generic. Think "premium fintech" with the personality of Stripe/Pitch. Strong typography, warm gold accents, high-contrast layouts. The gold (#C8962E) is the "Midas touch" — signature, not decorative.

## Design System
- **Framework**: Next.js 14 + Tailwind CSS
- **Fonts**: Space Grotesk (display/headings), DM Sans (body/UI), JetBrains Mono (code)
- **Primary accent**: Gold/amber — brand-500 (#D4941A), brand-400 (#F5B731)
- **Text colors**: ink-900 (#1C1917) through ink-50 (#FAFAF9) — warm stone tints
- **Surfaces**: surface-50 (#FAFAF7), surface-100 (#F5F2ED) — warm cream, not cold gray
- **Dark mode**: Class-based (`dark:` prefix) — ink-950 (#0C0A09), ink-900 (#111110), ink-800 (#1C1B19)
- **Border radius**: Default 10px (`rounded-[10px]`)
- **Components**: Custom (no UI library), lucide-react icons, framer-motion animations
- **Signature element**: `gold-bar` class — 40x3px gold accent bar used as section marker
- **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.2 AA target
- **Anti-patterns**: No gradient text, no animated blobs, no glassmorphism, no identical card grids

## Differentiation
The "Panda Score" — a multi-signal matching algorithm that scores jobs on 7+ dimensions rather than just keywords. The interface reveals this intelligence through match rings, heuristic breakdowns, and confidence indicators. The warm gold palette distinguishes it from every cold blue/purple SaaS template.
